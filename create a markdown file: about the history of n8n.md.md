# The History of N8N
=====================

N8N (Node-RED) is an open-source workflow automation tool that allows users to create workflows by connecting nodes. Here's a brief overview of its history.

## Early Days (2014)
--------------------

* Node-RED was first created in 2014 by Walter E. Brown, also known as "wscot".
* The project started as a simple JavaScript-based flow editor, allowing users to create workflows by connecting nodes.
* The name "Node-RED" comes from the fact that it allows users to connect nodes (or "nodes") together to create complex workflows.

## Growth and Development (2014-2016)
--------------------------------------

* In 2014, Node-RED was first released as an open-source project on GitHub.
* During this period, the community grew rapidly, with more contributors joining the project.
* The tool gained popularity among developers, makers, and IoT enthusiasts due to its ease of use and flexibility.

## Expansion and Adoption (2017-Present)
-----------------------------------------

* In 2017, Node-RED was acquired by Red Hat, a leading open-source software company.

* The tool continued to grow in popularity, with new features and improvements being added regularly.

## Community and Contributors

Node-RED has come a long way since its humble beginnings in 2014. From its simple JavaScript-based flow editor to its current status as a leading workflow automation tool, Node-RED continues to evolve and adapt to meet the needs of its growing user base.
